How to Play CloverPit

Choose your strategy wisely

Collect and use powerful charms

1. Basic Operations

  • Slot Machine Operation: Click to insert coins, match symbols (hearts, clovers) to earn coins, prioritize high-multiplier combinations.
  • Debt Management: Pay off randomly generated debt within the countdown each round, or your save slot will be permanently deleted.
  • Item Usage: Collect items like "Frenzy" and "Prosperity" to improve clearing efficiency. Some items require sequential combinations (trigger "Ambition" first, then stack "Miracle Reversal").

2. Advanced Techniques

  • After 20 consecutive failures, activate the "Fortune Resonance Protocol" to convert defeat data into global buffs.
  • Complete specific item collections (gather 5 lucky charms) or achieve hidden conditions (zero-debt escape) to unlock the freedom ending.
  • Endless mode challenges extreme high scores – balance risk and reward, avoid debt collapse from chasing high multipliers.

3. Using Items and Mechanics

  • Early game: Prioritize "Heart" symbols to quickly accumulate coins, avoid high-risk combinations too soon.
  • Item Combinations:
    • Frenzy (Yellow) + Prosperity (Green): Boost symbol multiplier and clearing efficiency for steady progress.
    • Ambition (Red) + Miracle Reversal: Reverse disadvantageous situations, activate before debt critical point.
  • If debt exceeds 80% of current coin reserve, consider abandoning the round to preserve your save slot.
  • Hidden Mechanism: Click the blood scab texture in the cell corner 3 times to trigger temporary debt relief.

Beginner's Guide to CloverPit

1. Key Items and Strategies

Item Combinations:

  • 150+ items carry "Karma Contamination Value" – excessive combo enhancement may summon materialized debt demons. Recommended combo: "Lucky Charm + Time Freezer" extends round duration for more operations.
  • Probability Deconstruction: Use specific items to reorganize slot symbols, triggering the cross-dimensional 777 super equation for massive coin gains.

Fortune Management:

  • Fortune Resonance: Accumulate negative fortune from first 20 defeats, trigger "Miracle Reversal" at critical rounds for exponential coin growth.
  • Heart Rate Control: Ending determined by player heart rate. Resting heart rate below 50 triggers "Cardiac Redemption" ending – adjust state through meditation or breathing exercises.

2. Advanced Techniques

Operation Optimization:

  • Ten-Grid Distance Positioning: When encountering high-burst enemies like Pepe, maintain ten-grid distance to avoid fatal attacks while using focused Mandy for counterattack.
  • Two-Star Counter Strategy: When facing two-star Pepe, actively counter to minimize losses. Combine with "Pseudo-Human" item for comeback potential.

Resource Allocation:

  • Coin Management: Prioritize paying high-interest debts to avoid ATM penalty mechanics.
  • Item Selection: Choose items based on current situation. Use "Time Freezer" under debt pressure, "Probability Deconstruction" when burst needed.

3. Multiple Ending Routes

Ending Trigger Conditions:

  • Cardiac Redemption: Triggers at resting heart rate below 50 – maintain calm during gameplay.
  • Miracle Reversal: Accumulate negative fortune through consecutive misfortunes, trigger one-in-a-million probability reversal at critical round.

Hidden Easter Eggs:

  • Domestic Violence Escape: In specific levels, find hidden items or solve puzzles to escape the front door.
  • Goat Pseudo-Human: Explore latest escape routes, trigger hidden storylines through environmental interactions.
